## 2. Typography & Color System

### 2.1 Typography Hierarchy
We utilize clean, high-legibility geometric sans-serif typefaces optimized for both digital mobile screens and high-density printed sheets (`mark sheets`, `A4 portfolios`):
- **Primary Interface Font**: `Inter` or `Plus Jakarta Sans` (`font-sans tracking-tight`).
- **Page & Section Headers**: Bold, tight-tracking weights (`text-2xl font-bold tracking-tight text-slate-900 dark:text-white`).
- **Monospace Code & Verification Badges**: `JetBrains Mono` or `ui-monospace` (`font-mono text-xs font-semibold`).

### 2.2 Color System & Accessibility
All UI colors must maintain high-contrast accessibility ratios (WCAG AA compliant across both Light and Dark modes):
- **Primary Brand Indigo/Blue**: `bg-indigo-600 hover:bg-indigo-700 text-white` (`used for primary call-to-action pills like [ 🚀 Publish to All ]`).
- **Success & Verification Green**: `bg-emerald-50 text-emerald-700 border-emerald-200 dark:bg-emerald-950/40 dark:text-emerald-300` (`used for VERIFIED_COVERED topics and ALLOWED gate pass cards`).
- **Warning & Attention Amber**: `bg-amber-50 text-amber-800 border-amber-200 dark:bg-amber-950/40 dark:text-amber-300` (`used for SELF_REPORTED_ONLY topics and unmapped subject warnings`).
- **Danger & Immutability Red**: `bg-red-50 text-red-700 border-red-200 dark:bg-red-950/40 dark:text-red-300` (`used for NOT_ALLOWED gate passes and FORBIDDEN deletion errors`).

## 4. Printable Document Standards (`ACHOLA ROSE` Format / `⌘P`)

Every printed artifact generated by NEYO (`Timetables`, `Mark Sheets EE.4`, `Digital Portfolios EE.14`, `Report Cards`) must strictly follow our standardized, chrome-free print engineering guidelines (`PrintTimetablePage` in `print-timetable-page.tsx` & `page.tsx`):
1. **Chrome Isolation**: Printed documents must live on dedicated routes outside `(app)` (`e.g. /print/timetable`) so sidebars, top navigation bars, and floating action buttons **never bleed onto printed sheets**.
2. **Exact Header Standardization**: Top header must center the official school title and year (`RATIBA YA SCHOOL MWAKA 2026`).
3. **Double-Period & Lunch Merging**: Consecutive periods of identical subjects double-merge (`colSpan={2}`). LUNCH and BREAK periods vertically merge (`rowSpan={days.length}`) into a single column (`never one cell per day`).
4. **Bottom Corner Signatures**: Remove generic text (`"Teacher timetable"`) from bottom-left; display clean generated timestamps (`Generated: <Date/Time>`). Bottom-right must display our non-removable brand hallmark: **`Powered by NEYO`**.
5. **Edge-to-Edge Print Layout**: Enforce default `daysVertical = true` landscape orientation (`margin: 6mm` exact edge-to-edge layout).

## 5. Voice and Tone Guide (The Bundi Rule)

### 5.1 Core Persona: High Enthusiasm, Exact Transparency, Direct Verification
When communicating across marketing copy, blog posts, system release notes, and customer support channels, NEYO speaks with:
- **Exact Transparency**: We never make vague marketing claims. We cite exact metrics (`e.g. "Verified 126/126 checks passing across 15 suites", "<150ms QR scanning speed"`).
- **Zero Jargon**: We talk about *classes, streams, marks, fee balances, and gate passes*—not microservices, pods, or LLM embeddings.
- **The Bundi Rule (`PROMPT 1`)**: **Never write the word "AI" in product copy.** If a feature automates a heavy workflow (`such as OCR exam tidying EE.5 or 1-click universal presets EE.15`), credit our digital assistant: *"Bundi is here to help"*, *"Bundi drafts..."*, or *"Ask Bundi"*.
