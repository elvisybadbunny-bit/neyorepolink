# NEYO Founder Manual V2 — Module 30: Master Operating Checklists

**Purpose:** turn Modules 01–29 into repeatable school and NEYO operating routines.  
**Last verified:** 2026-07-18

## 1. School opening setup — once

- School Profile: legal/display identity, levels, Day/Boarding/Both, contacts, logo/brand.
- Current Academic Term and dates.
- Modules enabled only where used.
- Users invited securely; roles/2FA; HR records.
- Classes/streams/capacity/class teachers.
- Subjects/departments/HODs/curriculum/pathways.
- Students/guardians/classes/subjects; import failures resolved.
- Payment credentials tested; fee structures.
- Timetable needs, periods/break/lunch/Saturday, teachers, venues, constraints, electives.
- Draft timetable reviewed/printed/published.
- Parent/student portal links tested.
- Backups, status, support contacts, security/privacy responsibilities.

## 2. Principal/Owner daily

- Dashboard exceptions; do not decide from summary alone.
- Attendance completion/absentees/unusual classes.
- Staff attendance/leave/substitutes.
- Fee collections/pending/suspense/cash.
- Admissions/interviews/offers.
- Discipline/clinic/security/panic incidents.
- Timetable/substitution/online class changes.
- Urgent parent/staff messages and delegated tasks.
- Assign owner/date and preserve evidence.

## 3. Teacher daily

- My Classes/today timetable/substitute alert.
- Register P/A/L/E and sync offline queue.
- Teach plan; mark truthful status.
- Record Work covered and CBE/assessment evidence.
- Homework/notes and submissions/feedback.
- Follow chronic absence/learning needs through approved channels.
- Confirm PTA/online class/cash responsibilities.
- Never retain learner data on shared device.

## 4. Reception daily

- Visitor sign-in/badge/host/student link; sign-out.
- Search before duplicate inquiry/student/payment.
- Walk-in cash/manual M-Pesa/STK real references.
- Admissions inquiry and call relay.
- Pickup/pass verification.
- Report-card day identity checks.
- Day-End Summary: on-site visitors, cash, calls, inquiries, unresolved items.

## 5. Bursar/Finance daily

- Provider callbacks/pending/suspense/duplicates.
- Cash/Reception/Teacher pending confirmations.
- Invoice balances/aging/promises.
- Discounts only with authority/reason.
- Reminder eligibility/quota/dedupe.
- Receipt/invoice print audit.
- Bank-clearing evidence.
- Activities/service charges connected once.
- Day-close cash/provider/ledger reconciliation.

## 6. Hostel/Clinic/Library/Transport/Cafeteria daily

- Hostel: beds/curfew IN-OUT-LEAVE/exeat/visitors/missing alerts.
- Clinic: allergies before treatment; visits/referrals/doses; confidentiality.
- Library: exact copy issue/return/condition/fines.
- Transport: route/shift/riders/requests/compliance/fuel/safety.
- Cafeteria: menu/headcount/allergies/stock issue/tables/queue/wallet.
- Every student charge posts once to Invoice.

## 7. Weekly school review

- Attendance trend/chronic/anomalies and guardian follow-up.
- Syllabus/Record of Work: verified vs self-reported.
- Homework/LMS participation and learning evidence.
- Fee leaderboard/aging/promises/reminders.
- Admissions pipeline/capacity.
- Staff leave/substitute/workload.
- Library/hostel/transport/inventory exceptions.
- Calendar next two weeks; targeted communication preview.
- Timetable warnings/changes; maintain Draft before Publish.
- Security: inactive staff, access, visitor/pass/pickup anomalies.

## 8. Monthly school review

- Reconcile all payment methods and statements.
- Payroll Draft review → Approve/Lock → payment/statutory evidence.
- Expenses/procurement/three-way matches/assets/stock.
- Parent accounts never logged in and portal adoption.
- Results/CBE/competency/portfolio moderation.
- Staff appraisal/training/discipline/contract expiry.
- Vehicle/driver/supplier/contract/maintenance expiry.
- Storage/print/SMS usage and quotas.
- User/access review; revoke departed sessions.
- Backup/export/incident/actions review.

## 9. Before exam

- Exam setup/subjects/max marks/classes.
- Exam timetable periods/venues/options blocks/student clashes.
- Invigilator pools; materials/completeness.
- Printable papers/mark sheets/tracking references.
- Privacy/copyright/national-sharing scope.
- Publish operational timetable; notify roles.

## 10. After exam

- Enter/scan marks; resolve uncertain deltas.
- Missing/over-max/subject/class scope.
- Means/positions/ties/stream-level comparisons.
- Reports sample/branding/QR.
- Request release → Principal Return/Approve.
- Publish; verify own-child portal.
- Archive materials; retain audit; follow learning weaknesses.

## 11. Term opening

- New current term/dates.
- Promote/graduate/reshuffle preview and capacity.
- Confirm classes/streams/students/staff/contracts.
- Teacher allocations/continuity/rotation flags.
- Subject choices/electives/pathways.
- Schedule rules/periods/blocked/time-off/venues.
- Generate Draft, review prints, Publish.
- Fee structures/invoice levels/services.
- Meal/transport/boarding plans/cards/invoices.
- Calendar/communication/onboarding.

## 12. Term closing

- Complete attendance/marks/CBE/Record of Work/syllabus.
- Release reports and portfolio packs.
- Finance aging/arrears/promises/reminders/reconciliation.
- Library/coursebooks/fines; hostel/asset damage; transport balances.
- Payroll/expenses/procurement close.
- Staff/learner transfer/graduation evidence.
- Data export/backup and term metric snapshot.
- Record issues/decisions for next term.

## 13. New academic year

- Backup/restore readiness.
- Promotion plan final classes/year; unknown levels resolved.
- Commit once; review PromotionRun/Undo window.
- Alumni/final labels.
- Reshuffle strategy preview.
- Allocate imported subject combinations/classes/capacity.
- Teacher allocation review/continuity/transfer.
- New term, fee, timetable, calendar, documents.
- Confirm parent/student access after movement.

## 14. NEYO Founder/Support daily and weekly

- Health/incidents/deployments/providers/jobs/webhooks.
- Central subscription payments/trials/grace/suspension.
- Support threads/demos/quotes/onboarding/compliance.
- Tenant Health/SMS/Storage/Bundi quota.
- Flags/pilots/maintenance windows.
- Build/test evidence; no false completion.
- Revenue/pipeline/cash/cost/discount/referral.
- Access/secrets/rotation.
- Customer interviews/actions/decisions.

## 15. Incident checklist

- Declare severity/commander/time.
- Contain without destroying evidence.
- Identify tenant/data/actions/provider/deploy.
- Preserve logs/audit/references.
- Stop unsafe jobs/callback repeats.
- DPO/legal/ODPC/school notification assessment.
- Restore/rollback/reconcile.
- Communicate next update.
- Add regression/monitoring/action owner.
- Post-incident review and Decision Log if policy changed.

## 16. Founder verification before declaring any module complete

- Intended role can complete real workflow.
- Denied role and wrong tenant blocked.
- Data saved in source record and downstream handoff works.
- Loading/empty/error/populated and mobile 360px.
- Offline/retry/idempotency where applicable.
- Print/PDF/QR/branding where applicable.
- Notifications/payment/provider outcomes verified, not assumed.
- Tests executed; environment blockers stated.
- Checklist/Anchor/manual updated and commit pushed.

Modules 01–30 now cover the planned operating sequence. Future work should update the owning chapter
when UI/code changes, fix any newly discovered gap full-stack, and add screenshots/task-level help
articles rather than creating duplicate contradictory manuals.
